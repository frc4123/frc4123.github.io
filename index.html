About Us

Our team brings together professional mentors with enthusiastic and passionate students from St. John Bosco High School and St. Joseph High School. Our mission is identical to that of FIRST as a whole:

"to inspire young people to be science and technology leaders, by engaging them in exciting student-based programs that build science, engineering, and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership."

Continue down to meet the team, see what we're currently working on, and get more information on what it means to be a member of Tribe Robotics!

Ingenuity & Creativity

As a robotics team we promote ideas that are made by the students. We put these ideas to the test and if they fail to complete the task the students are now challenged to come up with another idea. This is why creativity is important in our organization. Creativity is a necessity with the guidelines that FRC provides. The rules that we are given are seemingly limiting, but with ingenuity a robot can be made to overcome the challenges put forth and often done adeptly.

Teamwork & Unity

As a robotics team it is essential that communication is kept with the team. When working with others on the robot it is important to keep everyone informed. This allows for everyone to work fluently and with fewer conflicts. Working together also creates bonds between the members and we become a family. Together in unity is how to accomplish our goals.
